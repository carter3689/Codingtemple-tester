Why do I want to be a web developer?

I'll be honest, the industry I have worked in for so long tends to be rather fickle so job stability is a large motivator. However, as my hobbies will tell you, I'm a creative. I love building things myself and reflecting fondly on the effort as well as the finished product. I enjoy the idea of building something in the little sandbox (text editor) that can go out into the world and be useful to somebody as an app or webpage.

In addition, I have a desire to build apps and sites related to fitness as well as a deep love for video games, so I intend to expand my coding knowledge into that arena after developing a solid foundation at Coding Temple.
